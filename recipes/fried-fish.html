Fried Fish Recipe

Description

An amazing mouth watering fish that has been fried to perfection. would you like to have this amazing recipe to share with friends or just show off, your amazing skills in the kitchen? Continue reading to find out how to make this amazing fish! Not only will it taste amazingly but it can also show you how to properly descale a fish and serve it with other dishes.

Ingredients

  • Fresh Salmon
  • Sunflower Oil
  • 1 Table Spoon Sea Salt
  • 1 lemon sliced
  • 1 Table Spoon Black Pepper

Steps

  1. Descale the fish
  2. Season the fish with black pepper and salt
  3. Heat up sunflower oil and wait until its hot
  4. Fry the seasoned salmon until its golden brown
  5. Sprinkle the lemon to your liking
