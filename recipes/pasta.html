Pasta Recipe

Description

In this recipe we will show you how to cook pasta properly. Nothing taste better than a well cooked pasta meal, This recipe is simple and a starting point to make many pasta dishes. Once you know how to cook pasta properly you can start exploring other recipes.

Ingredients

  • Uncooked Pasta
  • 4 cups of water
  • 1 Teaspoon of Salt

Steps

  1. Start by boiling the 3 cups of water
  2. Place pasta in the boiling water, Add salt, and cook for 7min exactly
  3. Drain the water and rinse the pasta with cold water to remove any film
