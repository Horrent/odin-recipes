Chocolate-Cake

Description

Ingredients

  • Cocoa powder
  • Semi sweet chocolate chips
  • All purpose flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup of milk or milk alternative
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar

Steps

  1. Place cocoa powder and all purpose flour in a bowl
  2. Add 2 eggs and 1 cup of milk or alternative and whisk until smooth
  3. Add 1/2 cup of brown sugar and continue whisking
  4. Finally add semi sweet chocolate chips
  5. Now preheat oven to 370 and bake the dough for 40min
